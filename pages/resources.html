Rubik's Cube Resources

The World Cube Association

The World Cube Association is the official organization that hosts speedcubing competitions. You can create an account and sign up for competitions in your area and get official results. There are a variety of events you can sign up for, ranging from regular NxN cubes, blindfolded, pyraminx, square-1, and more! At competitions, you can meet world class cubers at competitions and make friends with fellow cubers.

cstimer

Cstimer is a free online Rubik's cube timer that comes with many features and customizable options. With cstimer, you can generate scrambles for any WCA puzzle and more, time your solves, and organize and save them in sessions. Cstimer also has built-in tools, like a scramble visualizer, time trend graph, other statistics, and many more.

Rubik's Cube Stores

There are a variety of stores that specialize in selling Rubik's cubes and other related twisty puzzles. There are several stores in the US, like TheCubicle and Speedcube Shop, but there are also stores in other countries; Australia has DailyPuzzles and Canada has CubingOutLoud. There are also many cube manufacturers, like Qiyi, Moyu, Gan, and YongJun, many of which are based in China. They have their own sites as well, but I mostly buy my cubes from online stores in my country.
